about dimzēni

Dweling house of Dimzēni was built in the begining of 20th century and is more than 300 square meters big. It was renovated in 2001-2004 trying to keep and to recreate authentic style and atmosphere of Latvian countryside as it was 100 years ago. Living conditions, of course, are better now (electricity, waterpipes.., even WC), but still differs from city standarts.

The best is to visit Dimzēni during weekends, when there is a sauna!

Biedrība Dimzēni

Dimzēni is an biological farm situated in the middle of Zemgale, 25 km from Jelgava on the left shore of river Svete. Farm has 11 ha of land, where 8 ha is used for sheparding goats and horses, and 3 ha is used for garden and farm's technical needs.
