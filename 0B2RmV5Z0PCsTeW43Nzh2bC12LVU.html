Biedrība Dimzēni

Dimzēni is an biological farm situated in the middle of Zemgale, 25 km from Jelgava on the left shore of river Svete. Farm has 11 ha of land, where 8 ha is used for sheparding goats and horses, and 3 ha is used for garden and farm's technical needs.
